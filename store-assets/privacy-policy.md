# ContextClip Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 2026**

## Data collection

ContextClip does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

## What the extension does with your data

- **Screenshots** are captured from the current tab and processed entirely on your local machine. They are either saved to a local folder you choose (via the File System Access API), your Downloads folder, or held temporarily in memory while you interact with the capture modal.
- **Clipboard** — the structured prompt text is written to your system clipboard when you click "Copy for Cursor". No clipboard data is read or transmitted externally.
- **Settings** (chosen folder name) are stored locally in `chrome.storage.local` on your device only.

## Network requests

ContextClip makes no network requests to any external URL. All operations are entirely local.

## Third parties

No third-party analytics, tracking scripts, or SDKs are included.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published in the GitHub repository at the homepage URL listed in the extension manifest.
